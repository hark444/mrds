CANCELLATION POLICY

A. For therapy bookings:

1. You may cancel or modify(switch therapy provider/ decrease therapy sessions / opt for a break in continuity of therapy) your booking any time through our website.

2. When you switch your provider your remaining package may credited in your My Refer wallet, which can be used in taking therapy from any other therapist subjected to acknowledgement from your therapist, but you have to make choice within 72 hrs otherwise it deemed cancelled.

3. When you opt for a break in continuity, you can halt therapy for a maximum of 7 days (only ones per package) only when your therapist confirm.

4. Cancellation request may only accepted when it was acknowledged by your therapist.

5. The applicable refund amount will be credited to you within 7 to 14 working days. We reserves the right to debit from your My Refer wallet, in case the cancellation amount being higher then money all ready paid by you.

6. i) For up to 14 days bookings:

- No Show: In case you not show up for your therapy session, charges for the one day therapy shell be deducted.

- Mid therapy cancellation/modification: In case you decided to shorten your all ready booked therapy sessions, you will charged one extra therapy session as cancellation fee.

ii) For package bookings:

- No Show: In case you not show up for your therapy session, 7% is deducted from total package cost.

- Mid therapy cancellation/ modification: In case you decided to shorten your all ready booked therapy sessions, 10% is deducted from Package cost left with you to avail.

B. For cancellation of Renewal of Subscription plans:

1.You may cancel renewal of your subscription at any time before due date of settlement through our website.

2. The applicable remuneration amount will be credited to you   within 7 – 10 days from the settlement date. We reserves the right to debit from your My Refer wallet, in case the cancellation amount being higher then money all ready paid by you.

i) for completed treatment packages till due date of settlement: your remuneration will be as per terms and conditions of your subscription.

ii) for completed treatment packages which fall after the due date of settlement: your remuneration will be 70%* flat.

 

*On completion of package by patient, otherwise it is calculated on number of sessions given by you to patient.

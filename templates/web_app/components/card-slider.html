Pain

Physiotherapy plays a crucial role in the management of pain. After a thorough assessment, a therapist will make a treatment goal together with you to help cure pain and treat the roots of your symptoms to prevent its reoccurrence.

Fracture and orthopaedic Physiotherapy

Physical therapy is a need of hour following immobilization. An early therapy technique after immobilization has been proven beneficial in increasing functional outcomes.

Paralysis, Bell's palsy and neuro physiotherapy

Physiotherapist treats neurological conditions with procedures which help to regain or maintain functional movement and independence.

CP and pediatrics physiotherapy

Physiotherapist have specialist knowledge in movement science, development therapies that are likely to affect the baby or growing child and help children to achieve their optimal physical development.

Ligament reconstruction and Physiotherapy for sports injuries

Helps in the prevention and treatment of injuries happened due to sport and exercise participation.

Frozen shoulder, knee pain and Geriatric physiotherapy

Covers a wide range of problems associated with old age like arthritis, osteoporosis, joint replacement, balance disorders, urinary incontinence, etc .we help senior citizens to be comfortable and pain-free.
